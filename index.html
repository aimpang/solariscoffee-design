Our Story

A little gem on St. Patrick

Solaris Coffee is a bright and cozy specialty coffee shop in downtown Toronto. We believe every cup should feel like a warm ray of light — carefully crafted, thoughtfully served, and made to brighten your day.


From our signature Honeycomb Latte to the beloved Tiramisu Latte, each drink is an invitation to slow down and savour the moment. Whether you're grabbing a quick pick-me-up or settling in, you'll find sunshine here.
